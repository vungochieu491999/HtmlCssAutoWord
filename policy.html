3.New Car Warranty.

No Warranty Terms:

1.Warranty shall not apply to any damage or loss that occurs in the event that a vehicle is not maintained periodically, inadequate and improper maintenance in the User's Guide and / or maintenance is not performed by an Authorized Agent.

2.Warranty shall not apply to any damage or loss caused by repair or adjustment not following the methods specified in the User's Guide and / or any damage. , any loss caused by a repair or adjustment is not made by an Authorized Agent.

3.Warranty shall not apply to any damage or loss caused by overloaded vehicles, operation of vehicles on inappropriate terrain, or operation of vehicles not in accordance with methods. specified in the User's Guide, or exceed the specified limit (maximum load, number of passengers, engine speed and other characteristics).

4.Warranty will not apply to any damage or loss caused by (i) not using genuine parts and accessories provided by the Authorized Agent; and / or (ii) use of fuel of the wrong type and use non-genuine oils, lubricants and chemicals provided by the Authorized Agent in accordance with the User's Guide.
The cases mentioned in (i) and (ii) may reduce the operability and reliability of cars and exempt from all related responsibilities.

5.Warranty will not apply to any damage or loss caused by the repair, modification, modification, modification, connection, disconnection, installation of spare parts, accessories,...

6.Warranty will not apply to all damages caused by the preservation of cars and transportation of cars not in accordance with the User's Guide; improper use of cars (racing, overloading, driving into flooded roads ...); accident, external force impact or other similar situations.

7.Wearing parts such as buggers, fuel filters, oil filters, wind filters, brake pads, clutch discs, lights, fuses, brushes, wipers, belts and parts of rubber, glass ..., are not covered by the warranty.

8.Solutions such as oil, grease, battery electrolyte solution, cooling water radiator ... are not covered by the warranty.

9.Cleaning, inspection, adjustment and other routine maintenance items. The costs incurred for the periodic inspection and maintenance of cars by the Authorized Agent shall not be within the scope of the warranty responsibility and shall be the responsibility of the owner.

10.Any expense to remedy the consequences of improper repair or maintenance that has been performed before will not be covered by the warranty. This repair cost will be paid by the Authorized Agent - where the repair and maintenance work is not done - paid, even if the warranty period has expired. Owners will not have to pay for these jobs.

11.Warranty shall not apply to any damage or loss caused by factors beyond the manufacturer's control such as a natural disaster (fire, earthquake, natural disaster, flood , thunderstorms, thunder, lightning, flooding ...); fire, collision, kick, theft or other similar situations and subsequent failures as a resolt of these events; damage, losses caused by smoke, chemicals, bird feces, sea water, sea breeze, salt, acid rain or other similar phenomena.
